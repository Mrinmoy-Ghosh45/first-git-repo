KPS INSTITUTE OF POLYTECHNIC, HOOGHLY

KPS INSTITUTE OF POLYTECHNIC, one of the leading Polytechnic Institutes in Hooghly, West Bengal was envisioned and developed by KPS Memorial Trust in the year 2015. The Trust is formally run by a group of educationists driven by the objective and zeal of spreading quality technical education to the meritorious students in rural India and set a benchmark in polytechnic education.

At KPS, we have equipped faculties and students with modern state-of-the-art infrastructure and advanced laboratories to train aspiring technical professionals with relevant skills and competencies. The campus is spread over 10 acres of land with Wi-Fi facility to facilitate students with ace internet connectivity that is one of the top requisites for building a futuristic technical professional. The updated polytechnic course curriculum along with hands-on learning experience of the students increases their employability in this fiercely competitive industry.

KPS Polytechnic Institute, a center par excellence is dedicated to impart progressive technical education with dedicated and experienced academic team, research orientation and rich industry interface. The institute has designed a dynamic learning environment which keeps the students abreast of all latest technological best practices across the globe and helps them to build successful careers in future.
